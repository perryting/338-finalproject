Location

Right at the heart of the happiest city in America.

HaHa Sushi + Grill is currently located at 1065 Olive St, San Luis Obispo, right off the Central California US 101 Highway. We're at the center of a city that has been dubbed the "happiest city in America" by locals, media, and travelers alike. Whether you are passing by for a road trip pit stop or are a San Luis Obispo local, HaHa is just one corner away from freshly prepared Japanese cuisine.
