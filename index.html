Fresh fish, prompt service, and a personal karaoke bar for your friends and family.

HaHa Sushi + Grill is a restaurant based in the sunny city of San Luis Obispo. With roots in the Japanese food industry for several decades, we believe that we serve the finest east-Asian cuisine on the central coast.

Our most popular dishes are sushi, whether it be signature crafted rolls or our $2 Tuesday value rolls. Sushi (すし, 寿司, 鮨) is a Japanese dish of specially prepared vinegared rice (鮨飯 sushi-meshi), usually with some sugar and salt, combined with a variety of ingredients (ネタ neta), such as seafood (most commonly and often raw), vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

If you didn’t grow up eating sushi, you may be confused when looking at a sushi roll menu if the restaurant chooses to leave out the descriptions. Our waiters and in-store menus will break down some common sushi terms and recipes so you can know what to order when visiting a sushi bar or restaurant. Feel free to contact us with any questions or make a stop by the restaurant!
